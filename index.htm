Introduction

You log in and something seems wrong. Perhaps your character is wearing something different or you are in a new guild. "Odd", you think. While the game loads you fantasize about the adventure you will have today with your friends and the great items you have yet to loot. Then when you log in it hits you: you have no gold. Your bank has been cleaned out and you have nothing but the clothes on your back (if you are lucky). You get a sinking feeling, an ill feeling, and perhaps a tinge of anger.

You have just been hacked.
